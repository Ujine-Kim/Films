Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Data Controller

Phenomenon Films (phenomenonfilms.art) is operated by an individual entrepreneur. Contact: info@phenomenonfilms.art

2. What Data We Collect

  • Email address — provided by you during sign-in, used for authentication and purchase confirmations
  • Purchase history — which films you have purchased or rented
  • Technical data — IP address, browser type, collected automatically for security and analytics

3. Purpose of Processing

  • User authentication (sending verification codes)
  • Providing access to purchased films
  • Ensuring security of the service

4. Legal Basis

We process your data based on your consent (Article 6 of Federal Law No. 152-FZ "On Personal Data"). You provide consent by checking the checkbox during sign-in.

5. Data Storage

Your data is stored on a secure server. We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties, except:

  • Email delivery service (Resend) — to send verification codes
  • When required by law

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise your rights, contact us at info@phenomenonfilms.art

8. Cookies

We use an authentication cookie (JWT token) to keep you signed in. This is a functional cookie required for the service to work. No tracking cookies are used.

9. Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted on this page.
